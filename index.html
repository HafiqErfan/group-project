About Us

Since 1963, The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf has been a pioneer in serving premium coffee and tea to communities worldwide. Founded in Southern California, we are committed to delivering the finest handcrafted beverages using the best quality ingredients sourced from across the globe. Our coffee beans are handpicked from the top 1% of Arabica beans, and our tea leaves are carefully selected from the world’s best estates to ensure exceptional flavor and quality. With a focus on innovation, sustainability, and community, we aim to create an inviting space where people can connect over a cup of coffee or tea. With over 1,000 locations in 30+ countries, The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf continues to bring joy to coffee and tea lovers with every sip.
